American Diplomacy, 1775 – 1825

The Early American Foreign Service Database (EAFSD to its friends) traces the U.S. Foreign Service from its early attempts at supplying the Continental Army during the American Revolution through the establishment of U.S. embassies in South America.

The EAFSD is both a public resource and my dissertation, which will be submitted to the Corcoran Department of History at the University of Virginia in the Summer of 2013.
